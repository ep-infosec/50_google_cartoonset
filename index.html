Cartoon Set  Home Download Team

Cartoon Set is a collection of random, 2D cartoon avatar images. The cartoons vary in 10 artwork categories, 4 color categories, and 4 proportion categories, with a total of ~1013 possible combinations. We provide sets of 10k and 100k randomly chosen cartoons and labeled attributes.

These cartoons helped develop the technology behind the personalized stickers in Google Allo. Download Allo to try it out!

We've also used the dataset to research cross-domain image translation: XGAN: Unsupervised Image-to-Image Translation for Many-to-Many Mappings.
